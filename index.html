Here are some interesting facts about Chameleons:

  • Almost half of all known species live in madagascar
  • Chameleons Mainly Change Color In Order To Communicate Or Regulate Body Temperature
  • Skin Crystals Enable Chameleons To Change Color At Will


Here are some interesting facts about Pandas:

    1. Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).
    2. Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.


Here are some interesting facts about Cicas:

Here are some interesting facts about Pigeons:

    1. Pigeons are highly sociable animals.
    2. Both female and male pigeons share responsibility of caring for and raising young.
    3. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
